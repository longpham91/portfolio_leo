Wreaths Across America Admin PortalM

Wreaths Across America (WAA) is a non-profit organization, aiming to honor and remember the fallen U.S. heroes.

This project's purpose was to build an Administration portal for WAA employers, contributors and volunteers to update their personal profiles, manage their staff and make donations. The Portal has proven to be a wide success and contributed remarkably to Wreaths Across America's efforts to raise awareness about Wreaths Across America Day, December 17th 2015, and put over 400,000 wreaths on the Arlington National Cemetery.

  • Client: Wreaths Across America
  • Start Date: October 2015
  • End Date: December 2015
  • Project Type: Front-End Development | UI Design

WAA Mobile Web Application

Wreaths Across America (WAA) is a non-profit organization, aiming to honor and remember the fallen U.S. heroes.

This project's purpose was to build a Mobile Web Application for WAA's donors and volunteers, so that they can easily make a donation via Credit Card or Electronic Check by entering secured information, or via Paper Check by taking an image and then safely voiding the check similarly. The Application has also proven to be a wide success and contributed remarkably to Wreaths Across America's efforts to raise awareness about Wreaths Across America Day, December 17th 2015, and put over 400,000 wreaths on the Arlington National Cemetery.

  • Client: Wreaths Across America
  • Start Date: October 2015
  • End Date: December 2015
  • Project Type: Web Development | UI Design | Mobile Design
